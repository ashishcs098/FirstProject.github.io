Elon Musk

He was the founder of the following companies:

  • Zip2 in 1995.
  • X.com in 1999 which turned into Paypal in 2001.
  • Tesla in 2003.
  • The Boring company in 2016.

Here are some of his other achivements:

  • Provided the initial concept and financial capital for SolarCity which was then co-founded in 2006 by his cousin.
  • On August 12, 2013,Musk unveiled a concept for a high-speed transportation system which we know as Hyperloop.
  • In December 2015,Elon Musk announced the creation of OpenAI, a not-for-profit artificial Intelligence (AI) research Company.
